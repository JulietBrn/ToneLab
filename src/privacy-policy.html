Privacy Policy

No Third-Party Sharing

We do not share your personal information with third parties unless required by law or with your explicit consent. Your data remains secure and confidential within our systems.

Data Retention Policy

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy or as required by law. Once no longer needed, your data is securely deleted.

ToneLab does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If we become aware of such data, we will take immediate steps to delete it.

We employ advanced security measures, including encryption and regular audits, to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.
